Why us

A genuinely good company with a genuinely silly name

Here's the joke, resolved: we called ourselves Probably Fine because every garage-door company in Greater Vancouver sounds identical — "trusted, reliable, same-day, licensed & insured." You can't tell them apart, and some of them are exactly the scam the BBB keeps warning about.

So we did the opposite. A name you'll remember, paired with the stuff that actually matters: Canadian-owned, trained technicians, upfront flat pricing, and a fix-it-right-or-we-come-back guarantee. We're so bad at upselling that we'll just fix the spring you actually need.

FAQ

Questions, answered honestly

Is the name a joke?

The name is the only thing we're not serious about. The work, the pricing, and the warranty are completely serious. We're not 'probably' fine — we're genuinely great, and the name just makes you remember us.

How much does a garage door repair cost in Greater Vancouver?

Most common repairs are flat-priced (tap Pricing in the footer for spring numbers): single spring (tap Pricing), two springs with free cables (tap Pricing), cable repair from $159, opener repair from $149. You always get a written quote before any work — no $19.99 bait, no surprises.

Can you come the same day?

Usually, yes — broken springs and stuck doors are our most common same-day calls across Metro Vancouver. Call or text early and we'll give you a real arrival window, not a vague all-day promise.

Are you actually licensed and insured?

Yes — licensed (municipal business licence), carrying commercial liability insurance, and WorkSafeBC-covered. Garage-door work is an unregulated trade in BC, so we tell you exactly what 'licensed' means rather than implying a certificate that doesn't exist.

What areas do you serve?

All of Greater Vancouver — Vancouver, Burnaby, Surrey, Richmond and Coquitlam plus the Tri-Cities, New West, the North Shore, Delta, Langley, Maple Ridge, Pitt Meadows and White Rock.

What if the repair doesn't hold?

Fixed right or we come back free. That part isn't a joke — every job carries a workmanship warranty and we stand behind it.
